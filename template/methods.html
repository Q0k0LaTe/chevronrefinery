How this project gathered data, selected visuals, and documented environmental justice stories in Richmond.

Research Approach

  • Mapped HOLC redlining layers against current demographic data.
  • Compiled refinery incidents from EPA ECHO and BAAQMD enforcement records.
  • Summarized peer-reviewed studies and community reports on health impacts.

Data sources: CalEnviroScreen 4.0, Mapping Inequality, Contra Costa Health, EPA, BAAQMD.
